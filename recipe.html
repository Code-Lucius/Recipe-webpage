Vegetable soup(Edikaikong)

Ingredients:

  • Waterleaf
  • Ugu
  • Palm oil
  • Stockfish
  • Beef
  • Pomo
  • Snail
  • Crayfish
  • Dry pepper
  • Maggi
  • Salt

Preparation steps:

Step 1: Thoroughly wash the beef, pomo and snail and set aside.

Step 2: Put the beef in a pot and season with maggi cubes, salt and finely diced onion, add little water then put on the fire. Boil till the meat is a bit tender then add the pomo to the boiling beef. Boil till both the beef and pomo are tender.

Step 3: Wash already sliced waterleaf and ugu leave and put in a pot(use a big enough pot as the soup will be cooked in this pot)

Step 4: Add the cooked beef and pomo along with the snail, crayfish, stockfish, pepper into the pot of vegetables and put on fire.

Step 5: When the content of the pot starts heating up, add a cube of maggi into the pot and stir.

Step 6: Add your meat stock to the pot and allow to heat for 2 minutes.

Step 7: Add suitable amount of palm oil to the pot.

Step 8: Taste for salt. Add if necessary.

Step 9: stir and turn off the gas.

Tips and Variations:

Tip 1: In the case where you have a very watery waterleaf, you might not have to add water. This commonly happens during the rainy seasons as leaves generally have more water during that period.

Tip 2: You might need to boil the stockfish a little. This is dependent on how soft/hard it is.

Variations: People always have different ways of doing things. This is my own way and I will definitely be open to learning other variations.
